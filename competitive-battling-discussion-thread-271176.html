Competitive Battling Discussion Thread | Bulbagarden

July 27, 2024
blog
The new trailer that debuted at the Pokemon World Championships yesterday revealed a lot of neat information on the topic of competitive battling, and I hadn't seen a Discussion Thread yet dedicated to competitive play so I figured this trailer definitely warranted one be made. New information we got includes:

Galarian Weezing's new Ability, Neutralizing Gas, which is stated to disable all other abilities, it was shown neutralizing a Gastrodon's Storm Drain and a Rotom's Levitate.

A few new Held Items, the "Room Service" which lowers the holder's Speed during Trick Room. The "Eject Pack", automatically switches a Pokemon out when it has a stat lowered, such as after using Overheat or Draco Meteor.

A new move was shown off, "Breaking Swipe", being used by Flygon, which hits both targets in double battles and lowers the Attack.

Corviknight's Hidden Ability, "Mirror Armor", reflects stat-lowering effects, such as from Growl or Intimidate. I personally believe Corviknight will be popular in both singles and doubles formats due to having a Gigantamax form AND such a strong HA.

Dynamax'd Pokemon are shown to be unable to be flinched, such as by Fake Out.

Max moves were revealed to have effects when used, such as raising a stat on your side of the team, or causing a weather condition or terrain condition. Really cool stuff.
