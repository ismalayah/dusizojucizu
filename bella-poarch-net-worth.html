What is Bella Poarch’s Net Worth?

Bella Poarch, a singer and social media personality, has a net worth of $9 million. She became famous in 2020 for a TikTok video in which she lip-synced to “M to the B,” making it the most-liked video on the platform. Poarch has over 90 million followers on TikTok as of mid-2022. Her debut single, “Build a Bitch,” was released in 2021.

Bella Poarch’s Early Life

Bella Poarch, born as Denarie Taylor on February 9, 1997 in San Fabian, Pangasinan, Philippines, was raised by her grandmother until the age of three. Later, she was adopted by an American father and Filipina mother. Poarch grew up on a farm with her adopted brother and two adopted sisters. However, her childhood was difficult as she faced physical and verbal abuse from her father.

At the age of 13, Poarch and her family moved to Texas.

Bella Poarch’s Military Service

Bella Poarch was influenced by her adoptive father, who was a US military veteran, to enlist in the US Navy in 2017. She served for three years, stationed in Japan and Hawaii.

Bella Poarch’s Rise to TikTok Fame

Bella Poarch’s TikTok account was created in early 2020, initially featuring gaming and cosplay content. However, she gained widespread recognition in August of 2020 for a lip sync video featuring rapper Millie B’s song “M to the B.” The video quickly went viral and became the most-liked video on TikTok. As of July 2022, Poarch had over 90 million followers, ranking her as the third-most followed person on the platform at the time.

Bella Poarch’s Music Career

Bella Poarch signed a deal with Warner Records in 2021 and released her debut single, “Build a Bitch,” which made it to number 56 on the Billboard Hot 100 in the United States and cracked the top-10 charts in India, Singapore, and Malaysia. The music video for the song featured fellow internet personalities Mia Khalifa, ZHC, Bretman Rock, and Valkyrae.

In August of 2022, Poarch released her debut EP, “Dolls,” which includes her previously released singles “Build a Bitch,” “Inferno,” and the title track, as well as new tracks “No Man’s Land,” “Villain,” and “Living Hell.” The EP also included music videos for the songs “Dolls” and “Living Hell.”

Bella Poarch’s Business Ventures

Bella Poarch released a limited clothing line in 2020 called RIPNDIP x Paca Collaboration. She also got involved with the professional esports organizations 100 Thieves and FaZe Clan, creating content for both. Poarch appeared on the 100 Thieves podcast “The CouRage and Nadeshot Show.”

Bella Poarch’s Personal Life

Bella Poarch married Tyler Poarch in 2019, but filed for divorce from him in late 2022. She currently resides in Los Angeles.

Bella Poarch Pays $400,000 Over Asking for Pacific Palisades Home

In April 2023, social media star Bella Poarch shelled out $4.3 million for a home in LA’s Pacific Palisades neighborhood. According to sources, she paid $400,000 more than the property’s asking price. The luxurious neighborhood is known for its stunning views of the Pacific Ocean and its A-list residents, including Ben Affleck and Jennifer Garner.
