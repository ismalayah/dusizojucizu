The V Battle Deck—Victini vs. Gardevoir is a pair of Sword & Shield Series Theme Decks released for the Pokémon Trading Card Game on May 7, 2021.

Blurb

V Battle Deck—Victini vs. Gardevoir

Two Strong Pokémon V Stand Ready!

Get ready for a major Pokémon battle with a ready-to-play double deck bundle—with extra Trainer cards! The Pokémon TCG: V Battle Deck—Victini vs. Gardevoir includes a deck for you and one for a friend, so you can face off. You can choose either Victini V or Gardevoir V to lead your team in a heads-up battle against another Trainer. Then, switch up the decks and see what things look like from the other side—can you win with both powerful Pokémon V?

Victini V Battle Deck

Burn Strong and Burn Bright—with Victini V!

Get ready to ignite a massive blaze and then keep Victini V well supplied with fuel for the fire! With Chatot picking up some extra Fire Energy and Cinderace grabbing more from the discard pile, you’ve got a Fire-type deck that knows how to burn quickly and keep the fires hot. Ninetales, Turtonator, and Scorbunny add extra heat to the Victini V Battle Deck!

Gardevoir V Battle Deck

All the Powers of the Mind—with Gardevoir V!

With the ability to turn damage into powerful attacks, Gardevoir V gives your opponent tough choices—and you have healing Items and other tricks to make it even harder! Suspicious Food Tin and Clefable keep the healing flowing, and Switch means your Pokémon come off the Bench at the right moment. Hawlucha and Dragapult bring their own attacks to keep the Gardevoir V Battle Deck smashing with total psychic power!

Information

The second wave of products in the V Battle Deck line included the Victini V Battle Deck and the Gardevoir V Battle Deck, which respectively focus on the Victory Pokémon Victini and the Embrace Pokémon Gardevoir. The titular Pokémon are featured as Pokémon V and have alternate artwork exclusive to each deck. The two decks could also be purchased together in the V Battle Deck—Victini vs. Gardevoir.

Each deck consists of 60 cards and come packaged with a deck box, damage counters, a single-player playmat, a rules booklet, a Quick Guide, three reference cards, an extra-large-sized coin, and a code card to unlock a digital version of it in the Pokémon Trading Card Game Online. The combined product includes eight additional Trainer cards, a guide to integrate them, and another TCGO code card.

Trivia

The Non Holofoil prints of Cinderace and Dragapult are unique to these decks. The Line Holofoil Professor Juniper print of Professor's Research was also initially exclusive to the combined product, but was later released in the V Battle Deck—Rayquaza vs. Noivern.
